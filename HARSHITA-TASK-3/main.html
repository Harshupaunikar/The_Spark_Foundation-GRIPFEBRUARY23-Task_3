ABOUT

A charitable donation is a gift of cash or property made to a nonprofit organization to help it accomplish its goals, for which the donor receives nothing of value in return. We are a small but mighty team, so we know what it’s like to work for a nonprofit organization with limited resources to achieve your mission. We’re here to help, with a powerful online fundraising suite, best in class customer support, and fundraising strategy resources to help you succeed.
